Why we want to help

Education equips individuals with critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving abilities, enabling them to tackle challenges effectively. It creates informed citizens who can participate actively in democracy and advocate for positive change. Furthermore, as we uplift ourselves through education, we collectively strengthen our nation, paving the way for a brighter future for generations to come. Embrace the opportunity to learn and be part of this transformative journey!

More about Empowering The Nation

Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to empower themselves and can provide more marketable skills.
The six-month programmes take place over 12 weeks and the short courses take six weeks.

Why we started all of this

In today's fast-paced world, empowering our nation begins with education. By investing in learning opportunities, individuals can acquire the skills and knowledge necessary to contribute to their communities and the economy. Taking courses not only enhances personal growth but also fosters innovation and collaboration, driving societal progress.
